## Q1: What is your idea?

1 in 4 students drop out for academic reasons, costing colleges $18K-$30K each. Boostability fixes it by breaking assignments into daily, doable tasks so students don't fall behind, and gives faculty a heads-up on who's struggling before it's too late.

---

## Q2: Who is it for?

Colleges buy it for retention. The students it helps are those historically underserved by higher ed: first-gen, non-traditional, and returning students who are capable but lack the structure to succeed. Faculty use the dashboard to find them before it's too late.

---

## Q3: What challenges will be solved with your idea?

Most struggling students don't need more content. They need structure. They procrastinate, miss deadlines, and disappear quietly. Meanwhile faculty have no way to know who's at risk until it's too late to help. Boostability solves the execution problem for students and the visibility problem for institutions, before dropout happens.

---

## Q4: How will you bring your idea to life?

Boostability already exists. We're not starting from scratch. Students use the mobile app to break assignments into daily tasks with SMS nudges to stay on track. Faculty get a real-time dashboard that flags who's disengaging. We've already piloted with 100+ users across 5 universities.
