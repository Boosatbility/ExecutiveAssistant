## Describe the Social/Environmental Challenges You Address

**The Problem**

Most students who drop out of college don't leave because they couldn't handle the material. They leave because nobody ever taught them how to learn.

**By the numbers:**
- 80-95% of college students procrastinate on assignments.
- 1 in 4 drop out for academic reasons.
- 1 in 5 drop out due to mental health.
- 20% have executive function challenges that directly affect their ability to manage a workload.
- The US economy loses $33B annually to preventable dropout.

**What the system misses:**

Faculty spend 6+ hours a week grading with no early warning visibility. Struggling students only become visible after midterms, when it is already too late to change the outcome. Academic support exists on most campuses, but it is underfunded, stigmatized, and built for students who already know how to ask for help.

The students who fall through are disproportionately first-generation, non-traditional, and adult learners. The populations higher ed was never originally designed to serve.

## Describe Your Solution

**The Solution**

Boostability is an AI-powered assignment management platform that directly addresses each part of the problem.

**Students never learned how to learn:**
- Boostability mobile breaks every assignment into daily, concrete tasks, removing the overwhelm of starting.
- SMS nudges keep momentum so students don't need to rely on motivation alone.
- Students build real execution habits over time, not just survive one deadline.

**Faculty can't see struggling students until it's too late:**
- Boostability 360 gives faculty real-time visibility into who is engaging and who is falling behind.
- Early warning flags appear weeks before midterms, when intervention can still make a difference.
- Works with existing tools, no new LMS or complex rollout required.

**Academic support doesn't reach the students who need it most:**
- Boostability is available 24/7, meets students where they are, and removes the barrier of asking for help in public.
- No stigma, no office hours, no waiting list.

**From our pilot (6 weeks):**
- Dropout rate: 44% historically, zero with Boostability.
- 100% midterm pass rate, 98% persistence.
- Organizational skills improved from 2/5 to 4/5.
- Student stress dropped from 7/10 to 4/10.

> "I was surviving, not living. Now I sleep without pills and feel confident." - S., Indiana University

## Single Biggest Challenge This Year

**Single Biggest Challenge**

The hardest thing in edtech is getting students to show up consistently. Students engage when they are in crisis and disappear when the pressure lets up. Building a real habit is hard, especially when using a new tool feels like one more thing to do.

**How we are preparing to overcome it:**

Our answer is reducing friction. Boostability integrates directly with institutions' existing Learning Management Systems, so students don't need to log into another platform or manually enter their assignments. Boostability pulls what they need and meets them where they already are. The less extra work it takes, the more likely students are to stay engaged, and that is where the real outcomes happen.
