#### What is your most significant skills need as a startup at the moment and how could skilled volunteers support you with it?

This is the most important question for getting the right volunteers. Be specific.

Think through Boostability's current gaps and pick the most urgent one. Options to consider:
- Product/UX design (improving student onboarding or feature flows)
- Marketing and student acquisition (content, social, partnerships)
- Tech development (specific features or infrastructure)
- Research (student behavior, learning science, competitor landscape)
- Outreach / partnerships (reaching universities, counselors, advisors)

Draft an answer that names the specific need, why it matters right now, and what a skilled volunteer could concretely do.

Be honest and grounded. Reviewers respect founders who know their risks. Frame around: the challenge, why it's real, and what Efrat is actively doing about it.
