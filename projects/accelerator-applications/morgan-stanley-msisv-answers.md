## 4. Product or Solution

**Question:** What is your product or solution? Describe your product in detail and what it does or will do. (Limit to 1000 characters)

Boostability is a student success platform built for the students who struggle most: first-generation, minority, neurodivergent, disabled, and returning adults. These students do not lack ability. They lack structure, accountability, and support that meets them where they are.

Our AI breaks overwhelming assignments into daily, doable tasks, builds personalized study plans tailored to each student's pace and learning style, and sends SMS nudges that reduce procrastination and rebuild the habit of showing up. Students stop spiraling. They start completing. They rebuild confidence through daily wins.

For faculty and academic leaders, Boostability 360 provides real-time disengagement alerts and early intervention dashboards. Faculty see which students are falling behind before they disappear. They step in early, with context, before a withdrawal becomes a dropout. Universities stop losing the students they were already failing to reach.

One platform. Two sides. Zero students left behind.

## 7. Revenue Model

**Question:** What is your revenue model? Be sure to answer the question 'How do you make money?' and describe revenue streams. (Limit to 1500 characters)

Schools pay us $12/student/month. That is it. We do not monetize student data. We do not sell to third parties. Schools are our only revenue source, and we think that is the right way to build this.

The fee breaks into two parts. $10/student/month powers the student app: personalized task breakdown, study plans, and SMS nudges that build accountability over time. $2/student/month powers Boostability 360, which covers everything the institution needs to make it work: student and faculty onboarding, faculty training, customized impact reports, and the integration layer that connects Boostability directly into the school's existing LMS so adoption actually happens.

Faculty and academic leadership get full access to Boostability 360 at no additional cost. This is intentional. The more faculty engage with early alerts and intervention dashboards, the better student outcomes get. Removing license friction from the people responsible for student success is how we make sure the platform actually works.

Schools start with a pilot cohort of 100 students. Year 1 revenue: 100 students x $12 x 9 months = $10,800. With $7,700 onboarding costs, first year margin is 28%. Year 2, the same school expands to 300 students with zero new acquisition cost: 300 x $12 x 9 months = $32,400. Margin jumps to 92%. One renewal funds the next school's acquisition.

We grow when schools renew. Schools renew when students succeed. That is not a coincidence. That is impact.
