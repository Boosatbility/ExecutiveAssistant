## Non-Traditional Definition (NCES Official)

A student with ONE OR MORE of these 7 characteristics:

1. **Delayed enrollment** - More than 1 year after high school
2. **Part-time attendance** - Not full-time enrollment
3. **Working full-time** - 35+ hours per week while enrolled
4. **Financially independent** - Per financial aid definition
5. **Has dependents** - Other than spouse
6. **Single parent** - Sole provider for dependent(s)
7. **Alternative credential** - GED or certificate instead of diploma

## SAM Definition

**SAM = Non-traditional students (age 25+) across all US higher education institutions**

Represents:
- The fastest-growing segment
- 34-37% of current market
- Primary offset to traditional student declines
- Underserved by traditional student success tools
