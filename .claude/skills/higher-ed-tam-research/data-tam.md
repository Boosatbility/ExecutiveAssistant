# TAM: Total Addressable Market

**US Higher Education - 2023**

## Market Size

| Metric | Count | Source |
|--------|-------|--------|
| Total undergraduates | 18.3M | NSC Research Center |
| Total postsecondary (incl. grad) | ~21.3M | NCES |
| Degree-granting institutions | ~4,000 | NCES |
| Four-year institutions | ~2,628 | NCES |
| Two-year colleges | ~1,268 | NCES |
| Institutions offering online | 3,640 (91%) | NCES, BestColleges |

## Market Definition

**TAM = All undergraduate students across all US higher education institutions**

All institution types:
- Public four-year
- Private nonprofit four-year
- Private for-profit
- Public two-year (community colleges)
- Online/distance learning institutions
- Professional schools
- Trade/vocational schools
- Certificate programs

All students regardless of traditional/non-traditional status.
