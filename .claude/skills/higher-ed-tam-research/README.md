## Key Statistics (At a Glance)

**TAM:** 18.3M undergraduates, 4,000 institutions

**SAM:** 5.9-6.3M students age 25+ (34-37% of market)

**Growth:** +19.7% YoY (first-year students 25+, fall 2024)

**Projections:** 35% of students age 25+ by 2030

**Why Now:** 13% decline in HS grads (2023-2041) + non-traditional growth
