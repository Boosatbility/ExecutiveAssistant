## What Reviewers Actually Score (4 Criteria)

1. **Relevance of Topic** - Does it matter to higher ed? Is it important/valuable?
2. **Outcome Alignment** - Do stated outcomes match what you actually teach?
3. **Quality of Submission** - Is it clear, comprehensive, well-researched, detailed?
4. **Engagement/Knowledge Transfer** - Will it spark discussion? Build new competency?
