## 5. MARKET DEFINITION: Non-Traditional Students (NCES Official)

Any student meeting ONE of these 7 characteristics:
1. Delayed enrollment (>1 year after high school)
2. Attends part-time
3. Works full-time (35+ hours/week) while enrolled
4. Financially independent
5. Has dependents (other than spouse)
6. Single parent
7. Earned GED/certificate instead of diploma

**Current Reality:** 74% of all students have at least one non-traditional trait
