## 3. Problem Statement
First-generation, minority, neurodivergent, and returning adult college students hit the same walls every semester: procrastination, overwhelm, missed deadlines, and no real support within reach. Traditional academic help requires self-advocacy most of them were never taught. 1 in 4 students cites academic struggle as a reason they left. Nearly 1 in 4 cites mental health. Universities lose $25,000 to $40,000 every time a student walks away. Nobody is solving this for them at scale.

---

## 4. Product or Solution
Boostability is a student success platform for struggling students. Our AI breaks overwhelming assignments into daily, doable tasks, builds personalized study plans tailored to each student's pace and learning style, and sends text message nudges that reduce procrastination and rebuild the habit of showing up. For faculty and academic leaders, Boostability 360 provides real-time disengagement alerts and early intervention dashboards. One platform. Two sides. Zero students left behind.

---

## 5. Technology Stack
Current: Bubble.io (no-code platform), GPT-4o for AI personalization and alerts, Twilio for text message nudges, Claude Code for development. Planned 2026-2027: learning management systems integrations (Canvas, CourseKey), native mobile app, data warehouse for analytics. Why: Bubble enables rapid iteration on real user feedback while maintaining security standards required for sensitive student data. We'll migrate to custom infrastructure at scale.

## 11. Pivots

**Pivot 1: ADHD-only to all struggling students**
Our pilot revealed academic struggle cuts across diagnoses. First-generation, minority, underprivileged, non-traditional, and returning learners faced similar barriers as ADHD students. Broadening our focus unlocked the institutional equity angle opening every school door.
Outcome: became a student success platform for all struggling learners.

**Pivot 2: Boostability 360 for early detection of struggling students**
Faculty and academic leadership shared a critical challenge: identifying at-risk students before midterm when intervention still works. They needed visibility into which students were falling behind, where momentum was dying, and which ones needed support before disappearing entirely. We built Boostability 360 to give them real-time visibility into early warning signs.

**Pivot 3: Boostability 360 assignment management integration**
Faculty needed to see the complete picture of assignment work as it unfolds in real time. They only learned when students reached out through office hours, a reactive channel that clogs their workload. Boostability 360 gives faculty proactive visibility into struggling students and intervention points before momentum dies.
